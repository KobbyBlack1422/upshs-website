University Practice Senior High School provides quality secondary education, extracurricular activities, and a supportive learning environment to prepare students for higher education and future careers.

To gain admission to University Practice, students must meet the Ghana Education Service (GES) standard after completing Junior High School (JHS). This includes passing the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) and selecting University Practice SHS as a preferred school during the placement process.
